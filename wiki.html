Anikha Surendran, also known as Baby Anikha, is an Indian actress known for her work in the Telugu, Malayalam, and Tamil film industries. She acted in Kadha Thudarunnu (2010), Yennai Arindhaal (2015) and Viswasam. She has received awards for acting. Anikha's debut as a child artist was in the film Kadha Thudarunnu in 2010. Subsequently, she has appeared in Tamil films like Yennai Arindhaal, and the web series Queen.In her first film Chotta Mumbai (2007), she had an uncredited role which appears just for some seconds in climax.
